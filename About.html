What is BTLED?

BTLED, or Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education, is a teacher education program designed to prepare future educators to teach Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE) in junior and senior high school.


MISSION: To guide organization members in their social and political aspects of life and to elevate them academic abilities and competencies, thereby helping them evolve into proactive and proficient students within the council.
VISION: An overarching student organization dedicated to carrying out responsibilities aimed at fostering the well-rounded personal growth of university students.
