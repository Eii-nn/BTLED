Fourth Year

On-the-Job Training (OJT)

Purpose: Hands-on experience in agriculture, fisheries, and TLE teaching.

Sites: Secondary schools, farms, hatcheries, DA/BFAR offices, TVET centers

Activities:

  • Planting, animal care, aquaculture
  • Farm tools handling and operations
  • Assisting TLE classes & demo teaching
  • Instructional material preparation

Requirements: Resume, endorsement letter, logbook/DTR, reports, evaluation form

Goal: Apply teaching and technical skills and gain real-world experience.
